# About Siddharth Kamboj

Siddharth Kamboj is a Solutions Engineer at Nutrient.io (formerly PSPDFKit), a software company that provides document software solutions to businesses. He's based in Pune, India, originally from Dehradun.

In ten seconds: a Solutions Engineer with a Computer Science undergrad and an MBA in IT, who loves both the technical side of building software and the people side of helping clients pick the right solution for their workflow.

Siddharth holds a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from Uttarakhand Technical University (2023) and is completing an MBA in IT from GLA University, Mathura (2025). His engineering work has spanned full-stack development with .NET Core, React, Angular, and Node.js, plus Chrome extension development from scratch.

Outside of work, he loves cooking, going to the gym, and trekking. He keeps up with current affairs, prefers nature to tall buildings, and spends time with family.
