# Solutions Engineer at Nutrient.io

**Company:** Nutrient.io (formerly PSPDFKit)
**Role:** Solutions Engineer
**Dates:** Part-time Feb 2024 – Nov 2024, Full-time Nov 2024 – Present
**Location:** Remote

Nutrient is a software company that provides document software solutions to businesses. Its flagship offering is the Web SDK — a JavaScript-based SDK for viewing PDFs, Office files, and images directly in the browser.

## What Siddharth does there

- Understands client needs and recommends the best Nutrient products for their workflow
- Addresses technical queries from prospective clients during the sales cycle
- Partners with Sales Representatives to communicate the technical capabilities of the Nutrient SDKs
- Facilitates seamless integrations of Nutrient products tailored to diverse client requirements
- Feeds client requirements back to the Product team — he's in direct contact with what clients actually need, so he starts new initiatives based on those signals

## Notable initiative

Siddharth initiated and built a new product offering on top of Nutrient's existing Web SDK technology to open a new revenue stream — a **documents solution for Salesforce** that brings PDF, Office, and image viewing into the Salesforce ecosystem to serve clients on that platform.
